# Four Platform Daily Content Example

User request:

```text
Turn this note into WeChat, Xiaohongshu, X, and Douyin versions:

Most people are not short of AI tools. They are short of reusable workflows.
```

Expected behavior:

- WeChat: explain the reasoning and give context.
- Xiaohongshu: make a saveable checklist.
- X: produce a sharp standalone opinion.
- Douyin: create a short, high-retention caption or script.

The agent should not simply shorten the WeChat draft four times.
