## Maintainer Statement

I am the primary maintainer of OrbitOS Agent Skills, an open-source collection of Chinese AI Agent skills for Obsidian knowledge management and multi-platform content workflows. The project turns repeatable personal and creator workflows into reusable `SKILL.md` instruction bundles that can be used by Codex-style local agents, Claude Code-style agents, and other compatible runtimes.

The repository currently includes three original skills:

- `saodust`: an Obsidian knowledge-base health check that scans inbox backlog, stale active projects, orphan notes, cold resources, dead wikilinks, and root hygiene.
- `write`: a structured Chinese long-form writing workflow that separates user words, external references, and AI synthesis.
- `four-platform-daily-content`: a Chinese multi-platform content workflow that adapts one source into WeChat, Xiaohongshu, X, and Douyin drafts.

I also maintain AI Home Design, a public AI prompt generator for whole-house customization and interior design workflows. It includes a Next.js web app, WeChat Mini Program version, Prisma/PostgreSQL backend, and prompt-generation assets.

I would use Codex to maintain these projects by reviewing skill instructions, improving examples, triaging issues, validating pull requests, detecting privacy leaks in public skills, improving documentation, and refactoring the product codebase.
